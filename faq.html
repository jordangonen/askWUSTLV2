Questions and Answers

For Freshmen

    Q: "What is your favorite part about being a freshman at WashU?"
    A: I love meeting new people every single day.

    Q: "What is your favorite food on campus?"
    A: I love to eat at the grill station in the DUC.

    Q: "Do you go out often?"
    A: I think there are tons of opportunities for students to go out and explore the city of St. Louis.

    Q: "Do you play sports?"
    A: I love going to the gym to play basketball with my friends.

    Q: "Is there a lot of free tutoring?"
    A: There are so many support groups for freshmen that truly want to help students excel in school.

    Q: "How is the workload?"
    A: To be honest, school is really hard.

For International

    Q: "What is your favorite part about being an international student at WashU?"
    A: The people are here are great about helping me around campus.

    Q: "What is your favorite food on campus?"
    A: I love to eat at the international station in Bear's Den.

    Q: "Would you recommend bringing a bike?"
    A: I get around on campus without the use of a bike - not really needed.

    Q: "Do you play sports?"
    A: The football field is really nice!.

    Q: "What are professors like? "
    A: They really want to help.

    Q: "Any tips"
    A: Meet as many people as you can.

For Transfers

    Q: "Hardest part about transferring?"
    A: It is not easy meeting new people every day.

    Q: "How are the dorms"
    A: The dorms are amazing!!!.

    Q: "What is the social life like?"
    A: People like to have a lot of fun, you just have to find your group of people.

    Q: "Any advice for transfers?"
    A: Get invovled in as many clubs as possible.

    Q: "Schedule advice?"
    A: Premed is really hard.

    Q: "How many hours a week do you have class"
    A: Most peopel take 15 credits a semester.
